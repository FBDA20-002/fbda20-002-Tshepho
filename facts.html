JAN VAN RIEBEECK

FACT 1

It was founded in 1652 when Jan van Riebeeck of the Dutch East India Company based in The Netherlands arrived to set up a halfway point for ships travelling to the East.

CLIMATE

FACT 2

Cape Town’s climate is on par with California and the Mediterranean. The winter season starts in May and ends in September and is generally a wet and windy season.

CAPE TOWN CASTLE

FACT 3

Cape Town’s Castle of Good Hope is the oldest building in South Africa. It was built between 1666 and 1679 by the Dutch East India company.

ENVIRONMENT

FACT 4

The Cape Floral Kingdom is home to 7000 plants found nowhere else in the world
